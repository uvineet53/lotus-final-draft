LOS ANGELES, a sprawling Southern California city and the center of the nation’s film and television industry. Near its iconic Hollywood sign, studios such as Paramount Pictures, Universal and Warner Brothers offer behind-the-scenes tours. On Hollywood Boulevard, TCL Chinese Theatre displays celebrities’ hand- and footprints, the Walk of Fame honors, offering myriad attractions and activities: movie studios, the Sunset Strip, Venice Beach, landmark museums, world-famous theme parks, wineries, breweries, outdoor activities, exciting nightlife, and much more to explore..

LOS ANGELES COUNTY MUSEUM OF ARTS:- This is the largest art museum in the western United States. It attracts nearly a million visitors annually. It holds more than 150,000 works spanning the history of art from ancient times to the present. In addition to art exhibits, the museum features film and concert series. It's a good spot to bring first time visitors LACMA houses more than 120,000 objects across different media, region and periods such as Greek, Roman and Etruscan art, Asian art, American and Latin American art, decorative arts and design, photography, and modern and contemporary art.

THE GETTY CENTER:- The Getty Center, in Los Angeles, California, is a campus of the Getty Museum and other programs of the Getty Trust. The $1.3 billion center opened to the public, is well known for its architecture, gardens, and views overlooking Los Angeles.

UNIVERSAL STUDIOS HOLLYWOOD WALT DISNEY CONCERT HALL:- Walt Disney Concert Hall is one of the most acoustically sophisticated concert halls in the world and provides both visual and aural intimacy through its exterior and interior design.For tourists its worth visiting.

DISNEYLAND PARK:- The Haunted Mansion, Matterhorn Bobsleds and It's a Small World. It's also the only Disney Park to feature New Orleans Square - one of Walt's favorite lands. It is home to iconic rides such as Pirates of the Caribbean. Here you can visit; walt studios park, disneyland paris, world of disney etc.

GRIFFITH PARK:- With its beautiful Art Deco architecture, planetarium, and educational exhibits, like the Tesla coil and Foucault pendulum, this place is worth a private observatory tour all on its own. The jewel of the park is, of course, the Griffith Observatory.

NATURAL HISTORY MUSEUM OF LOS ANGELES:- But we can show you the whole world at the Museum and online. Explore nature and culture from the inside out. Oh, we love dinosaurs. The American Museum of Natural History is the largest natural history museum in the world. Its collection totals over 30 million items.
