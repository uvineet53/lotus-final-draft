THAILAND, home to Buddhist temples, exotic wildlife and spectacular islands. It is also known for its fascinating history, unique culture and delectable local food. The tourism industry plays an important role in the Thai economy. Thailand truly has a chaotic charm to it that attracts people from across the globe. The country, also referred to as the Land of Smiles, never disappoints.

THE GRAND PLACE:- In the capital city of Thailand Bangkok, stands this royal ornate and expansive structure . Visually breathtaking and splendor filled, the grand palace is also where the Emerald Buddha aka Jade Buddha is found displayed. The Grand Palace is in fact a complex of buildings and has been the King of Siam's official residence. As a tourist this place is worth visiting.

KHAO YAI NATIONAL PARK:- It is home to protected habitats for the greatest number of animal species in Thailand. Its food sources attract many charismatic species which can often be viewed by visitors: wild elephant, gaur, dhole, muntjac (barking deer), sambar deer, gibbon, hornbill and macaque.

KHAO SOK NATIONAL PARK:- Khao Sok National Park is perhaps most famous for the bua phut (Rafflesia kerrii) flower. The top attractions to visit in Khao Sok National Park are:

  • Khao Sok Elephant Sanctuary
  • Pakarang Cave (Coral Cave)
  • Win Elephant Tour Khao Sok
  • Khao sok ATV Adventure
  • Aunchalee Thai Massage
