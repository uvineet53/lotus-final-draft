MALDIVES, famous for a number of reasons, including its vibrant culture, pristine beaches, sprawling greenery, and fascinating landmarks. Apart from the numerous enchanting landmarks, Maldives is also famous for water sports and other adventure activities that attracts visitors.

MAAFUSHI:- Maafushi is a great local island option if you just have couple of days to enjoy or to visit because they have better infrastructure and it is near the airport. Easy to access: Maafushi to become a popular destination for tourists visiting local islands.

CONRAD MALDIVES RANGALI ISLAND:- Situated on two interconnected islands in the Indian Ocean, Conrad Maldives Rangali Island is a one-of-a-kind resort that sits in perfect harmony with the natural environment. An untouched slice of paradise. Revel in our pools, with mesmerizing Indian Ocean views. Find bliss in your island sanctuary. Discover private gardens, plunge-pools, and stairs leading into the Indian Ocean.

ALIMATHAA:- Alimathaa Island has white-sand beaches and clear waters that tempt tourists from all over the world. If you are interested in exploring marine life comprising colourful coral reefs and a wide variety of fishes (eels, jack fish, and moray), then this destination in the Maldives is an ideal choice.

NATIONAL MUSEUM:-The National Museum of Maldives is located at Sultan Park in Male. It houses several well-kept artefacts of Maldivian culture, including coins, royal antiques, furniture, items of clothing, coins, embellishments and armaments.

COMO COCOA ISLAND:- It is a home to 33 overwater villas. Encircled by Coral. Located a Short 40 Min Speedboat Ride from the Airport. Ideal for Quick Maldives Escape.
