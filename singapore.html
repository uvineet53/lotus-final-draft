Your Journey Starts Here

SINGAPORE,Another awesome destination that every tourist dream to visit.It is filled with so much to explore. Singapore is famous for being a global financial center, being among the most densely populated places in the world, having a world-class city airport with a waterfall, and a Botanic Garden that is a World Heritage Site.

GARDENS BY THE BAY:- Wonder blooms at Gardens by the Bay. Invigorate your senses with our towering Supertrees, spectacular indoor waterfall, and iconic cooled conservatories — engineering and architectural marvels that enable plants from around the world to thrive in the tropics. Its home to Gardens by the Bay's most iconic structures and a great location for unforgettable vistas.

MERLION PARK:-A visit to singapore won’t be complete without selfie with the MERLION. This Half-lion, Half-fish icon is a mythical creature that symbolizes the city-state and its roots.

CHINATOWN:-This beautiful district is the only Chinatown in the world to boast a Buddhist temple, mosque and Hindu temple along a single street. Your journey into Singapore's religious traditions begins at the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple & Museum.As well as good variety of food.fascinating peek into Singapore’s Chinese culture and history, Chinatown is good place to start.

SINGAPORE FLYER:- At 165 metres tall, Singapore Flyer Located in the heart of downtown Marina Bay, the Singapore Flyer is Asia's largest giant observation wheel. More importantly, it is high on thrills for tourists and locals alike. Step into one of the 28 fully air-conditioned glass capsules, and be transported on a 30-minute journey of stunning day and night scenes.

SAND SKYPARK OBSERVATION DECK:- The Sky Park has the world's longest elevated swimming pool which is 146 meters long. The three towers that are built have a Theatre Block, a Convention and Exhibition Facilities Block, and a Casino Block, which includes more than thousand games and slot machines for the visitors. Located on the roof of Marina Bay Sands.

MONTEVERDE CLOUD FOREST:- In the Reserve there are more than 3,200 species of plants, including 700 species of trees and 500 species of orchids. Approximately 425 species of birds, 120 species of mammals, 60 species of amphibians and 101 species of reptiles also inhabit this forest.Cloud Forests are unique ecosystems, they only represent less than 1% of the remaining forests on the planet's surface. Here you can find very particular species of trees, plants and animals, such as the numerous species of orchids.

MARINA BAY:- Located in the Central Area of Singapore, surrounded by the perimeter of four other planning areas, the Downtown Core and Straits View. The area surrounding the bay itself, also called Marina Bay, is adjacent Central Business District. Besides housing and the most fantastically designed buildings.Marina Bay is one of the tourist attractions buzzing art scene, options for dining, shopping & entertainment, water sports, and river cruises.
